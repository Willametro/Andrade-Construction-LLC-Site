Our Story

Built on Family, Built on Craft

Andrade Construction LLC was founded in 2008 by Rudy and Pam Andrade with a simple idea: treat every client like a neighbor and every home like your own. What started as a small remodeling operation has grown into one of the Salem area's most trusted names in residential construction.

Over the past 15+ years, we've completed hundreds of projects across Marion, Polk, Yamhill, and surrounding counties — from single-room bathroom updates to whole-home transformations. We've built our reputation one project at a time, and we plan to keep it that way.

Rudy leads the work on-site every day, while Pam manages project coordination, client communication, and scheduling. When you hire Andrade, you're dealing directly with the owners.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Andrade Construction LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates andrade.construction. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill out our contact or estimate request form (name, phone, email, project details)
  • Call or text us at (503) 999-9277
  • Email us at andraderudypam@yahoo.com

We also automatically collect certain usage data via analytics tools (see Analytics section below), including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide estimates
  • To schedule and coordinate your project
  • To send you project updates and follow-up communications
  • To improve our website and services

SMS / Text Messaging

If you opt in to SMS communications, we will send you messages related to your project inquiry, estimate scheduling, or project updates. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help. We do not share your mobile number with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics

Our website uses the following analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our site:

  • Cloudflare Web Analytics — privacy-first analytics that does not use cookies or collect personal data.
  • Google Analytics 4 (via Google Tag Manager) — collects anonymized usage data. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • Meta Pixel (via Google Tag Manager) — used for advertising measurement. You can manage ad preferences at facebook.com/settings.

Cookies

Our site uses minimal cookies. We use localStorage to remember your language preference (English or Spanish). Google Analytics and Meta Pixel may set cookies for analytics and advertising measurement purposes.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business (such as Formspree for form delivery), provided they agree to keep it confidential.

Data Retention

We retain contact and project inquiry information for as long as necessary to provide services and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at andraderudypam@yahoo.com or (503) 999-9277.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
